About StayWell Hotel

Warm hospitality, modern comfort

Our Story

StayWell Hotel began as a family guesthouse with a simple promise: treat every guest like family. Over time we expanded into a full-service hotel while keeping that same warmth and attention to detail.

Mission & Values

  • Deliver consistently excellent guest experiences
  • Maintain high standards of cleanliness and safety
  • Empower staff through training and recognition
  • Operate sustainably and respectfully within our community

Sustainability

We prioritize energy- and water-saving measures, responsible F&B sourcing, and waste reduction practices across the property.
